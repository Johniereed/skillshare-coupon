How Do I Cancel Skillshare Subscription?

Cancelling your Skillshare subscription is a breeze, and we're here to walk you through the process.


First, log in to your Skillshare account on a desktop or laptop computer. Once you're logged in, head to your "Account Settings" page and look for the "Payments" section.


From there, you should see an option to "Cancel Subscription." Click on that, and Skillshare will walk you through the rest of the steps.


It's important to note that if you cancel your subscription, you'll still have access to Skillshare's premium content until the end of your current billing cycle.


After that, your account will be downgraded to the free version, which still gives you access to a limited number of courses.


If you're not sure whether you want to cancel or just take a break, Skillshare also offers the option to "Pause" your subscription. 


This will freeze your account for a set period, allowing you to come back and pick up where you left off without losing your progress.
